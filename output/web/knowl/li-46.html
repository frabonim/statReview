Item 2.

Using the sample data and assuming the null hypothesis is true, calculate the value of the test statistic. Again, to conduct the hypothesis test for the population mean \(\mu\text{,}\) we use the \(t\)-statistic \(t^*=\frac{\xbar-\mu}{s/\sqrt{n}}\) which follows a \(t\)-distribution with \(n - 1\) degrees of freedom.
