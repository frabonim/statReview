Item 4.
Set the significance level, \(\alpha\text{,}\) the probability of making a Type I error to be small -- 0.01, 0.05, or 0.10. Compare the \(P\)-value to \(\alpha\text{.}\) If the \(P\)-value is less than (or equal to) \(\alpha\text{,}\) reject the null hypothesis in favor of the alternative hypothesis. If the \(P\)-value is greater than \(\alpha\text{,}\) do not reject the null hypothesis.
