In our example concerning the mean grade point average, suppose that our random sample of \(n = 15\) students majoring in mathematics yields a test statistic \(t^*\) equaling 2.5. Since \(n = 15\text{,}\) our test statistic \(t^*\) has \(n - 1= 14\) degrees of freedom. Also, suppose we set our significance level \(\alpha\) at 0.05, so that we have only a 5% chance of making a Type I error.
