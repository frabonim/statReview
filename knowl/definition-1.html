Definition 2.2.1. \(t\)-Interval for a Population Mean.

The formula for the confidence interval in words is:
\begin{gather*} \text{Sample mean} \pm (\text{t-multiplier} \times \text{standard error}) \end{gather*}
and you might recall that the formula for the confidence interval more precisely is:
\begin{gather*} \bar{x}\pm t_{\alpha/2, n-1}\left(\dfrac{s}{\sqrt{n}}\right) \end{gather*}
